📜 RULES

Objective

Survive the dealer. Reduce his health to zero before yours hits zero.

Health

Both players start each round with 5 lives. Watch the wire cutter — if your health drops too low, it cuts your wires and you can no longer heal.

Rounds & Items

Round 1: No items. Pure probability. Shells loaded and you're told how many are live vs blank.

Round 2: 2 items per loadout. The game shifts to resource management.

Round 3: 4 items per loadout. All items available including rare ones.

On Your Turn

  • SHOOT YOURSELF — Blank = survive + extra turn. Live = lose HP, turn passes.
  • SHOOT DEALER — Blank = nothing, turn passes. Live = dealer loses HP.
  • USE ITEMS — Use before shooting to gain advantages.

Items

  • 💊 Healing Bottle — Restore 1 HP (useless if wires cut).
  • 🔍 Magnifying Glass — Peek at the current shell.
  • 🍺 Beer — Eject the current shell without firing.
  • 🪚 Handsaw — Double damage (2 HP instead of 1).
  • 🔗 Handcuffs — Skip the dealer's next turn.
  • 💉 Adrenaline — Steal dealer's item and use it immediately.
  • 📱 Burner Phone — Reveals a future shell (not the current one).
  • 🔄 Polarity Inverter — Flips the current shell: live↔blank.

Dealer AI

The dealer is strategic. He tracks shell counts, uses items, and adapts. Watch his behavior — he's not random.
